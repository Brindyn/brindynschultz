How to play: After the roll, choose how many dice from all players of a specific die face have been rolled, then press bid. Each player will either raise the bid by 1 or, if they think the bid is too high, they can challenge the player's bid before them by pressing the challenge button. This will reveal the dice to everyone. If the challenge was too early, the person that challenged loses and the bidder wins, otherwise, the last bidder loses and the challenger wins.
