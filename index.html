My interest in electronics stems from the hardware used in videogame consoles that I played with as a child. My father always reminded me that I had better be careful with my things, because if they break, they're gone for good. When those things inevitably did break, I opened them up and taught myself how they work so that I could repair them. As I got older, I often found myself criticizing their designs, and thought I could make something better, so I decided to go to school to learn how to design electronics.
